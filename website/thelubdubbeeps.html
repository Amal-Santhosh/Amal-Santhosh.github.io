The 'Lub-Dub' Beeps

Better than the 'lub-dub'
The beep made my heart ponder
Over the phone.
Seeing your name above all notification
Was nothing else than the drop
Carrying oxygen for survival
What my eyes seek were
Printed on the screen with beauty
And love from where it comes from.
Excited every neurons, the way
Which carries the words, and
The whole life when it is reached.
