Closely Far

Many times there is no need for them both to come,
Close, to see; to feel; to embrace.
Along the bank of the same river they stood,
Close, but unknown to each other.
Looking through every drop flowers carried,
Of the last night's rain; for the other,
They saw the same eyes looking at them.
They kissed;
Carrying the depth of all emotions, love;
Far from each other; kissing each other.
