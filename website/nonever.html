No, Never

No pen moves under same head in different papers easily, holded by same thumb.
No sky carries both the rays of sun and moon with it, at the same time.
No hand will hold the other around the wrist, if it was woven together once.
'No' was terrible at times when we parted for long,
Even more, when you were not mine near me.
But
No eyes will stop gazing from miles,
No smile will stop with deepened wrinkles,
No miles will part us within.
